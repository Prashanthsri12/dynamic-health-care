In-House Diagnostic Services

Advanced testing performed and reviewed by our specialist team.

EEG (Electroencephalogram)

Records brain electrical activity. Used to diagnose epilepsy, sleep disorders, and encephalopathy.

EMG (Electromyogram)

Evaluates the electrical activity of muscles to identify neuromuscular diseases.

NCS (Nerve Conduction Study)

Measures how fast electrical signals move through nerves. Detects carpal tunnel syndrome, neuropathy, and nerve damage.

Sleep Study (Polysomnography)

Full overnight sleep monitoring to diagnose sleep apnoea, restless leg syndrome, and other sleep disorders.

BiPAP / CPAP Titration

In-clinic or at-home device calibration for patients with sleep apnoea.
