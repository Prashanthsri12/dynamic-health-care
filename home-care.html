Creative Home Care

The Clinic Comes to You

Through our group company Creative Home Care, we extend our clinical services into your home. Whether you need medicines delivered, a test done at home, or specialised equipment for recovery — we make it happen quickly, reliably, and with the same quality you expect from Dynamic Health Care.

Section A

Medical Equipment at Home

Hospital-grade equipment delivered and set up at your home by trained staff. Rental and sales options available.

Oxygen CylinderFor patients needing supplemental oxygen at home.
Oxygen ConcentratorContinuous oxygen supply without refills. Ideal for chronic respiratory patients.
WheelchairsManual and motorised options for mobility assistance.
BiPAP / CPAP MachineFor sleep apnoea and respiratory support at home.
Nimbus MattressAnti-bedsore alternating pressure mattress for bed-ridden patients.
Pulse OximeterNon-invasive monitoring of blood oxygen levels.
Manual / Motorised BedHospital-grade adjustable beds for home use.
Cardiac MonitorContinuous ECG and vitals monitoring for cardiac patients at home.
Suction ApparatusFor clearing airways in patients with tracheostomies or secretion management needs.
Syringe PumpFor accurate, controlled IV medication delivery at home.
VentilatorFor patients requiring respiratory support. Setup and training provided.
Holter Monitor24-hour ambulatory ECG recording device.
DVT Stockings with PumpSequential compression therapy to prevent deep vein thrombosis post-surgery.
