About Dynamic Health Care

A decade of compassionate, specialist care for Chennai families.

Home›About
Our story

Built on Trust, Driven by Care

Dynamic Health Care was founded by Dr. R. Prithviraj with a singular vision: to make specialist-level healthcare accessible, affordable, and compassionate for families in Mogappair and across Chennai. What began as a general medicine clinic has grown into a multi-specialty practice with three expert doctors, in-house diagnostic facilities, and a thriving home care arm — all under one roof.

We believe healthcare is deeply personal. Every patient who walks through our doors is treated as a member of our extended family — with time, attention, and the expertise they deserve.

Our Values

The principles that shape every consultation, every procedure, every interaction.

Patient First

Every decision we make is guided by what is best for the patient. We listen before we diagnose.

Expertise with Empathy

Our doctors combine deep clinical expertise with warmth and genuine care for your wellbeing.

Accessible Healthcare

We are committed to making specialist care accessible, affordable, and close to home.

Our group

The Creative Dynamic Health Care Group

Five entities working together to deliver seamless end-to-end healthcare.

Dynamic Health Care

Primary outpatient care, specialist consultations, in-house diagnostics and treatment in Mogappair, Chennai.

Creative Dynamic Health Care

Occupational Health Centre (OHC) providing medical services in factories and industries across Chennai. Comprehensive health coverage for industrial workers.

Creative Scans and Labs

Full diagnostic imaging and lab testing — ultrasound scans, blood investigations, and a sample collection centre in Villivakkam, Chennai.

Creative Pharmacy

Two branches (Mogappair West and Villivakkam) supplying a wide range of prescription and OTC medicines, with home delivery across Chennai.

Creative Home Care

Rental and sales of essential home care medical equipment including oxygen concentrators, wheelchairs, hospital beds, CPAP/BiPAP machines, and more.

Meet Our Doctors

Three specialists, one shared commitment: care that puts you first.

Dr. R. Prithviraj

M.B.B.S., M.E.M. General & Emergency Medicine

Dr. Prithviraj is a specialist in General and Emergency Medicine and the founder of Dynamic Health Care. With his M.E.M. (Master of Emergency Medicine) qualification, he brings advanced emergency care training to the clinic alongside his broad general medicine practice. He is known for his meticulous diagnostic approach and his ability to put anxious patients at ease.

He also oversees the Occupational Health services provided to industrial establishments through Creative Dynamic Health Care.

Dr. S.V. Priyanka

M.B.B.S., M.S.(OBG), R.C.D. Gynaecologist & Obstetric Surgeon

Dr. Priyanka is a Gynaecologist, Obstetrician, and Surgeon with an M.S. in Obstetrics & Gynaecology and an R.C.D. certification. She specialises in high-risk pregnancy, infertility management, laparoscopic gynaecological surgeries, and comprehensive women's health from adolescence through menopause.

Her gentle, patient-centred approach has made her a trusted name for women across Chennai.

Dr. K. Sankara Subramanian

M.D., D.M. (Neurology) Consultant Neurologist

Dr. Subramanian is a Consultant Neurologist with an M.D. in General Medicine and a D.M. in Neurology. He specialises in the management of stroke (including thrombolysis), epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, and neuromuscular disorders.

He leads the advanced neurological diagnostic services at Dynamic Health Care including EEG, EMG, NCS, and sleep studies.
