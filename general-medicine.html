Whole-person care

Treating People, Not Just Symptoms

At Dynamic Health Care, our General Medicine department takes a whole-person approach to healthcare. Led by Dr. R. Prithviraj, we focus on diagnosing and treating the full spectrum of internal diseases — not just the presenting symptom, but the complete picture of your health.

We serve patients of all ages and genders, and we coordinate seamlessly with our specialist colleagues in Gynaecology and Neurology when needed.

Department Highlights

Specialised programmes built around the conditions we see most often.

Internal Medicine OPD

Daily outpatient consultations covering complex multi-system problems.

Emergency & Acute Care

Rapid assessment and management of acute medical emergencies, supported by trained emergency physicians.

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term management of diabetes, hypertension, respiratory illness, and lipid disorders with personalised care plans.

Preventive Health Packages

Tailored health check packages for all age groups, including routine blood panels, ECG, and lifestyle counselling.

A Special Focus on Geriatric Care

We understand the unique healthcare needs of older adults. Our geriatric care programme focuses on managing multiple chronic conditions simultaneously, improving mobility and quality of life, and reducing hospital admissions through proactive monitoring.
