# Carousel Images

The homepage carousel rotates through **10 photos** kept in this folder.

## How to replace

1. Prepare your photo (recommended size: **1200 × 675 pixels**, JPG, under 300 KB).
2. Save it with one of the filenames `carousel-1.jpg` through `carousel-10.jpg`.
3. Overwrite the placeholder file in this folder with the same name.

That's it — the carousel will pick it up automatically on the next page load.

## Tips

- The carousel cycles through them in order 1 → 2 → 3 … → 10 → back to 1.
- To change the order, rename the files (e.g. swap `carousel-2.jpg` and `carousel-5.jpg`).
- Keep all 10 slots filled. If a file is missing, the carousel will show a grey panel for that slot.
- 16:9 landscape orientation looks best (most modern phone photos are this ratio).
- Use **Squoosh** (https://squoosh.app) to compress your JPGs without visible quality loss.

## What works well

- Interior shots of the clinic — reception, examination rooms, the diagnostic area
- Doctors with patients (with consent)
- Staff team photos
- The pharmacy and home-care equipment
- Activities like a health camp or community event
